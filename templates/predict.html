ℹ️ Feature Guide

Chest Pain Type

TA: Typical Angina, ATA: Atypical, NAP: Non-Anginal, ASY: Asymptomatic

Resting BP

Blood pressure at rest (mm Hg). Normal: 90-120

Cholesterol

Serum cholesterol (mg/dl). Desirable: < 200

Max HR

Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise. Normal range: 60-202

Oldpeak

ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest

ST Slope

Slope of the peak exercise ST segment
